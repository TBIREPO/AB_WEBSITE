ABOIN Privacy Policy

At Aboin, we value your trust and are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to ensure its security.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

When you use the Aboin platform, we may collect the following personal details:/p>

  • Name, email address, phone number, and date of birth.

  • Family member details (e.g., name, contact, relationship).

  • Payment details, including billing addresses and transaction history.

b. Location Data

To provide location-based services, we may collect:

  • Real-time GPS data for service locations.

  • Service city and state entered during registration.

c. Health Information

For medical services, you may provide sensitive health information, such as:

  • Patient conditions, medical requirements, and hospital details.

d. Technical Data

We collect non-personal information, such as:

  • IP addresses, device types, operating systems, and browser details.

  • Usage data like page views, clicks, and app interaction logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Facilitate the booking of services such as medical assistance, property care, and personal help.

  • Communicate updates about bookings, payments, and service statuses.

  • Improve app performance, user experience, and service offerings.

  • Verify user identities for security and fraud prevention.

Health information is strictly used to match users with suitable healthcare assistants and medical vendors.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with:

    a. Service Providers

    • Personal assistants, medical vendors, and drivers to fulfill bookings.

    b. Legal and Regulatory Authorities

    • To comply with legal obligations or in response to valid legal processes.

    c. Third-party Vendors

    • Payment gateways for processing transactions securely.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data.

  • Secure servers and restricted access protocols.

  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information through your account settings.

  • Request the restriction of certain data processing activities.

  • Withdraw consent for data collection at any time.

For assistance with these rights, contact our support team at [Insert Contact Email].

6. Retention of Data

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. Once the data is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without verifiable parental consent.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember user preferences.

  • Analyze app performance and usage.

  • Serve personalized content and recommendations.

You can manage cookie preferences through your device settings.

9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The updated policy will be posted on our app and website with a revised effective date.
